Smart toy car for kids

As a first year Industrial design team, we designed an electric smart car for kids to be ridden in shopping malls. We used certain design elements such as human factors for ergonomic standarts for kids, electronics for sensors and buttons on the fully functioning prototype and 3d printing for the outside cassis.

Pasta maker toy for kids

As a first year Industrial design team, we designed a pasta maker that can make three different pasta by rotating a lever in different inputs. We have made a non functioning foam prototype and a functioning sheet metal prototype with a complex gear mechanism that translates a turning lever into pushing dye.
